-The only rule is that it must end in a different position
from the position it started, thus covering at least
one square it did not previously cover.
-Neutral pieces can be moved anywhere on the map, as long
as it does not cover the other player's piece.



























-In order to do that, right click on which neutral piece you
want to move and it will be highlighted. Then using the left
 mouse button, choose where exactly you want it to be moved.
-The player who stalemates his adversary wins.
